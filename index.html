Creative Brief

An unnamed fragrance exists before language. No logo, no title card, no VO explanation — only architecture, gesture, and scent implied through ritual. The film reads as a lost European broadcast: austere, provocative, unmistakably Parisian.

Casting centers on MADAME_X — tall with Helmut Newton curves (full bust, strong hips) who occupies space like furniture in a Newton interior — she is never performed, only observed. Two Dobermans function as sentinels and witnesses, not props. Medical instruments appear as luxury objects: stainless trays, glass syringes, latex gloves — the clinic as boudoir, Lagerfeld's irony made spatial.

Visual Codes

  • Lagerfeld: monochrome, structured shoulders, tweed, pearls, fingerless gloves, high collars, architectural hair, sunglasses as armor
  • Newton: mansion corridors, marble checkerboard, gilt mirrors, low voyeuristic angles, hard side light, sexual tension through distance
  • Bottle: faceted crystal silhouette (Chanel N°5 echo), entirely unlabeled — light and refraction carry identity
  • Animals: Dobermans / greyhound — still, alert, slightly threatening calm
  • Medical: steel tray, glass syringe, white gloves — precision as seduction, never horror

Narrative Arc

Act I · Arrival

Shots 01–03. Mansion awakens. Woman enters power. Dogs watch. Bottle introduced as relic.

Act II · Ritual

Shots 04–07. Application becomes ceremony. Mirrors multiply gaze. Medical props elevate intimacy.

Act III · Afterimage

Shots 08–10. Macro bottle. Corridor departure. Black hold — scent without name.

Production Notes for Portfolio

  • Cast: one lead 180cm+, androgynous-powerful bearing; two trained Dobermans with handler on set
  • Location: Paris 16e hôtel particulier or Budapest standing set with marble + gilt
  • Lens package: 35mm / 50mm / 85mm macro — all shot wide open for Newton softness at edges
  • Grade: true B&W, no sepia; retain specular cruelty on skin and glass
  • Legal: spec ad — no Chanel trademark, no Lagerfeld likeness; codes only
