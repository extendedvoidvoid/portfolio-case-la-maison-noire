## Prompts

### 01 — Almost known

Lily-Rose Depp exact face from reference: luminous dewy skin soft wing liner natural deep lips. 16:9 fashion film still. Soft grey seamless almost empty. She sits half in shadow half in soft key light looking just past camera. Black simple slip or fine black lace strap. Photo-shoot appetite. High fashion B&W with soft luminous skin. No dogs no mansion cliché. Unlabeled crystal bottle out of focus far background optional. Spec portfolio.

### 02 — Double truth

Same Lily-Rose Depp face exact. 16:9. Her face reflected twice in glass or split by refraction through clear crystal — know me / don't. Soft luminous makeup. Intimate close-up. B&W fashion. No logo text on bottle. Spec portfolio.

### 03 — Hands near glass

Same woman hands and partial face edge. Fingers near unlabeled faceted crystal perfume bottle. Soft luminous light on skin and crystal. 16:9 B&W fashion still. No brand type. Spec portfolio.
