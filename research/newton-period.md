# Helmut Newton period (1976–1981) — visual grammar

**Use for:** Acts I–II (shots 01–07 primary), body/casting, camera ethics.  
**Not:** soft lifestyle beauty, pastel, smiling demo, runway-thin casting.

## Period to respect

| Work | Year | What we take |
|------|------|----------------|
| *White Women* | 1976 | Fashion world as stage for power + voyeurism; viewer implicated |
| *Sleepless Nights* | 1978 | Fashion as crime-scene / clinic still life; steel, braces, medical props as luxury objects |
| *Big Nudes* / *Sie Kommen* (Dressed) | 1981 | Monumental tall self-assured women; Paris; dressed power equal to undressed power |

## Codes to keep

- **Body:** tall ~178cm, full bust, strong hips, long legs — Helmut Newton curves, never runway-thin  
- **Attitude:** documented, not performing; cold self-possession  
- **Camera:** high voyeur angles, partial obstruction, low imposing angles, hard side light, deep shadows  
- **B&W:** high contrast, specular cruelty on skin and glass  
- **Space:** mansion corridor, marble checkerboard, staircase, gilt mirrors  
- **Clinic-as-boudoir:** steel tray, glass syringe, latex gloves as still life — precision = seduction, never gore  
- **Witnesses:** dogs (Dobermans or greyhound — never both types in one shot) + architecture  
- **Ambivalence:** empowerment and voyeurism held in the same frame (DW: photos force the audience to grapple)

## Do not

- Soft beauty commercial smile  
- Product packshot with logo  
- Thin model cliché  
- Horror medical (blood, gore)  
- Soft fill that kills Newton shadow  
- Color fashion (Equilibrium is B&W; rim light exception only where L’Eau codes allow)
