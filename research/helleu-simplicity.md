# Jacques Helleu — simplicity as radical luxury

**Use for:** shots 08–10 (ending), theme of unlabeled bottle, interview talking point.

## Core idea (WWD)

Chanel image longevity through continuous rejuvenation; keystone = **simplicity** (laid down with the first N°5 advertising logic).

Helleu’s lineage: cinematic and fantasy fragrance advertising; égéries as mythic faces — but for this **spec**, we abstract further: **no name, no logo, no title card**.

## Codes for La Maison Noire

| Code | Application |
|------|-------------|
| Scent before name | Bottle is faceted crystal silhouette only — identity = shape and light |
| Simplicity | Remove explanation; cut VO; cut supers |
| Abstract woman | MADAME_X is archetype, not named égérie |
| Afterimage | She leaves; scent (memory) remains |
| Black hold | Final luxury is refusal to brand |

## Interview line (job offer)

> “Helleu’s rule of simplicity: the film ends before the logo. The woman is the story; the bottle is shape and light. That is concepteur-rédacteur craft for Parfums Beauté — not a packshot.”
