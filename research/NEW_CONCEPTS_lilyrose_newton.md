# CONCEPT A — « Tu me connais / Tu ne me connais pas »

**Logline:** A young woman is filmed as if she already belongs to the image system — then the film admits the camera is lying about knowing her. Scent is the only true intimate.

| Layer | Choice |
|-------|--------|
| Newton | Voyeur structure without mansion cliché: **distance, partial frames, keyhole/window, her indifference** |
| Lily-Rose | Campaign paradox as **plot**, not caption |
| Product | Bottle appears late, unlabeled, almost accidental |
| Tone | Luminous B&W + one gold-magenta rim on glass only |

**10 beats (compressed):**  
01 Reflection without full face → 02 She looks at lens / looks away → 03 Hands close to glass → 04 Spray (no clinic) → 05 Direct gaze hold → 06 Fabric/breath CU → 07 Night glass (window = architecture accomplice) → 08 Bottle light only → 09 Empty frame she left → 10 Black  

**Value charge:** known → unknown → known only as scent  

**Why for the job:** proves you can **rewrite a house line** into a film structure (not decorate a moodboard).

---

# CONCEPT B — « Le regard est le produit »

**Logline:** The film never sells a bottle first — it sells the **complicity of looking**. She knows she is being watched; the perfume is what remains after the gaze fails to own her.

| Layer | Choice |
|-------|--------|
| Newton | Core ideology: **viewer is guilty**; power drama without saddle/brace |
| Lily-Rose | Photo-shoot appetite + modern face as subject who returns the look |
| Product | Unlabeled crystal; no N°5 type |
| World | Soft anonymous interiors / city glass — **not** hôtel particulier checklist |

**Key visual rule:** every shot has a “witness” that is **not** a dog — mirror edge, phone black glass, hotel door spyhole, elevator chrome, window reflection of the crew light.

**Why for the job:** pure **films & images** thinking — the CR idea is the gaze, not the set dressing.

# CONCEPT C — « Contrôle / lâcher »

**Logline:** From controlled beauty (posed, perfect, “campaign ready”) to a private ritual where control softens — scent as the moment the image system loses her.

| Layer | Choice |
|-------|--------|
| Newton | Power + submission **without fetish props** — only posture, stillness, then release |
| Lily-Rose | Public face vs private self (same person, two temperatures of light) |
| L’Eau | Watered radiance → then shadow |
| Ending | Helleu black: no name |

**Act structure:**  
I Control (posed, hard light, she performs “known”)  
II Crack (movement, breath, spray, imperfect)  
III Absence (she gone; light on empty glass)  

**Why for the job:** classic fragrance psychology (mask / skin) in CR language.

# CONCEPT D — « La femme documentée » (Newton pure, face Lily-Rose)

**Logline:** She is not performing a fragrance ad — she is **documented** in a cold European night. The bottle is evidence, not a hero packshot.

| Layer | Choice |
|-------|--------|
| Newton | “Documented not performing”; hard light; night streets / hotel corridor **without** tweed army |
| Lily-Rose | Face is specific, contemporary, not Amazon archetype |
| Anti-pastiche | No Dobermans, no medical tray, no checkerboard manifesto |
| Product | One shot only, unlabeled, on a night table like a personal object |

**Why for the job:** shows you can separate **Newton’s ideology** from **Newton’s props** — smarter for a senior CR.
