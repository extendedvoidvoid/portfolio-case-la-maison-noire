## Who decides the aesthetics (web research)

### 1. House DA — Parfums Beauté (primary)

| Person | Role | Why he matters for 2016 |
|--------|------|-------------------------|
| **Thomas du Pré de Saint Maur** | Head of **Global Creative Resources** / art direction for **Chanel Fragrance, Beauty, Watches & Jewellery** (Paris) | Decides advertising, films, social, campaign image for **Parfums Beauté** — the job-side equivalent of “who owns the beauty image.” |

Public bios (SHOWstudio, Alain Elkann Interviews, L’Officiel, Hunger, El País culture, etc.) place him as the long-running creative head of fragrance & beauty image — campaigns and content for the division for well over a decade (covering the 2016 L’Eau wave).

**For interview:** he is the **institutional answer** to “who decides Chanel makeup/perfume DA,” not the film director of one spot.

### 2. Film execution — N°5 L’Eau 2016 (secondary)

| Person | Role | Scope |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Johan Renck** | Director — *L’Eau N°5, the Film* with Lily-Rose Depp (2016) | **Execution** of one film: shot language, pace, youth energy. Not house DA of all beauty. |
| **Olivier Polge** | In-house perfumer — N°5 L’Eau juice | **Scent**, not makeup/image DA. |
| **Krister Linder** | Music (L’Eau film OST) | Sound only. |

### 3. Fashion image lineage (context, not Parfums boss)

| Person | Role | Scope |
|--------|------|--------|
| **Éric Pfrunder** | Image director / later artistic director for **fashion image** (from 1983; Karl’s image partner) | Fashion ads, photo culture, Lagerfeld image machine — **couture/fashion**, not the Parfums Beauté creative desk. |
| **Karl Lagerfeld** | Fashion creative director (to 2019) | House gravity; e.g. picked Lily-Rose for eyewear (2015). Fashion silhouette & codes bleed into beauty casting, but **Parfums campaigns sit under du Pré de Saint Maur’s division**. |

### Org logic (simple)

```
Thomas du Pré de Saint Maur  →  Parfums Beauté / fragrance & beauty IMAGE (DA)
        ↓ commissions
Johan Renck (etc.)           →  one film / one campaign execution
Olivier Polge                →  juice
Pfrunder + Karl              →  fashion image & house codes (tweed, pearl, black)
```
