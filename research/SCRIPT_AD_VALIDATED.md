# Film script — validated (text only)

**Working title:** *Tu me connais*  
**Format:** 16:9 · ~60–75s · B&W with rare luminous edge  
**Audio:** almost silence · one sparse piano motif · room tone · one spray · trip-hop-adjacent sparseness (Massive Attack *space*, not a cover)  
**VO / super / brand name:** **none**  
**Product:** **discreet only** — travel/personal scale (≈5–10 cm on set), never giant hero bottle, never label readable, **never logo / monogram**  
**Cast:** one woman (face lock: approved 2016 still — private research)  
**Cine grammar ref:** Madonna × Massive Attack *I Want You* (1995, dir. Earle Sebastian) — waiting room, wordless duration, refusal ending — see `REF_madonna_massive_attack_i_want_you.md`  
**Status:** script v1.1 — logo ban + I Want You narration locked  

---

## 1. Creative promise (1 sentence)

A young woman waits inside an image that claims to know her; she fills the wait with private gestures; when the “call” of the gaze finally lands, she refuses it. What remains is a trace of scent — not a name.

*(Narration cousin of *I Want You* video: Dorothy Parker waiting → hang up; here waiting → black / empty room.)*

## 5. Script — picture continuity (FR + EN)

**Duration target:** 70s · 10 shots · hard cuts except 09→10 dissolve optional  

### Beat 01 — 0:00–0:07 · *Presque connue / Almost known*
- **Image:** Medium. Soft grey void. She sits, half shadow. Looks **past** the lens.
- **Sound:** Room tone.
- **Newton:** Documented, not performing.
- **Bottle:** **absent**.

### Beat 02 — 0:07–0:13 · *Double / Double*
- **Image:** Close. Her face in a window or mirror; a second, softer reflection — not a gimmick logo shot.
- **Sound:** Faint piano note.
- **L’Eau paradox:** two truths, no title card.
- **Bottle:** **absent**.

### Beat 03 — 0:13–0:18 · *Le cadre regarde / The frame watches*
- **Image:** High or partial frame (door edge, glass edge). She crosses, indifferent.
- **Sound:** Silence swells.
- **Newton:** Architecture / frame as accomplice — without palace checklist.
- **Bottle:** **absent**.

### Beat 04 — 0:18–0:26 · *Geste / Gesture*
- **Image:** MCU hands + wrist. She takes a **small** unlabeled flacon from a plain surface. One spray. Intimate, not clinical.
- **Sound:** One precise spray.
- **Bottle:** **first appear — discreet scale only**.

### Beat 05 — 0:26–0:34 · *Elle regarde / She looks back*
- **Image:** Tight portrait. Eyes to lens. Long hold. She knows she is seen.
- **Sound:** Piano motif, unresolved (3 notes).
- **Newton + L’Eau:** return of gaze; photo-shoot appetite without smile-for-brand.
- **Bottle:** **absent**.

### Beat 06 — 0:34–0:40 · *Peau / Skin*
- **Image:** Macro lace or fabric at collarbone; breath. No face required.
- **Sound:** Soft fabric / breath (felt).
- **Bottle:** **absent**.

### Beat 07 — 0:40–0:48 · *Nuit / Night*
- **Image:** She at a night window; city soft bokeh. Cold exterior, warm cheek.
- **Sound:** Distant city.
- **Newton:** night documentation, not set-dressing luxury.
- **Bottle:** **absent**.

### Beat 08 — 0:48–0:56 · *Trace / Trace*
- **Image:** ECU **small** crystal on table or in hand edge-of-frame — light on facets only. Her presence reduced to a lip edge or pearl **optional**, not a face packshot.
- **Sound:** Near silence.
- **Helleu:** light before name; **no type**.
- **Bottle:** **second & last appear — still discreet**.

### Beat 09 — 0:56–1:04 · *Après / After*
- **Image:** Same window or chair. Empty. Residual light.
- **Sound:** Heels or cloth off-screen, fade.
- **Bottle:** **gone**.

### Beat 10 — 1:04–1:10 · *Raccroche / Hang up*
- **Image:** Her **hand** hangs up an **old-school hotel phone** (rotary / classic handset, coiled cord). Refusal. Optional brief black hold after cut.
- **Sound:** Receiver click → silence. **No super. No URL. No name.**
- **I Want You / Parker:** the call ends because *she* ends it.

## 6. Full script (dialogue-free shooting script)

```
TITLE: TU ME CONNAIS
FORMAT: 16:9 / ~70s / B&W
NO DIALOGUE / NO SUPER / NO BRAND NAME

1. INT. SOFT VOID — DAY (DIFFUSED)
   Woman, seated. Half shadow. She does not smile.
   She looks past us.
   HOLD.

2. INT. GLASS / MIRROR — CONT.
   Her face; a second reflection, slightly wrong, slightly late.
   We almost know her.
   CUT.

3. INT. THRESHOLD — CONT.
   Partial frame. She crosses. Indifferent to the camera.
   The frame watches her more than she watches us.
   CUT.

4. INT. QUIET ROOM — CONT.
   Hands. A small unlabeled flacon — personal scale.
   One spray on the wrist.
   Not a demonstration. A private gesture.
   CUT.

5. INT. CLOSE — CONT.
   Eyes into lens. Long hold.
   She knows she is seen.
   CUT.

6. INT. MACRO — CONT.
   Lace / skin / breath.
   CUT.

7. INT./EXT. WINDOW — NIGHT
   She at the glass. City beyond.
   Documented, not staged-pretty.
   CUT.

8. INT. DETAIL — CONT.
   Small crystal. Light only.
   No readable type.
   Optional: edge of lip or pearl — not a face sell.
   CUT.

9. INT. WINDOW / CHAIR — CONT.
   Empty. Afterimage of presence.
   CUT.

10. BLACK.
    Hold.
    END. No card.
```
